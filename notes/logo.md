# FosterHub Logo Notes

## Current visual reference

### Main Logo

An image reference was provided showing:
- a black background
- the wordmark **FosterHub** in dark gray
- the tagline **Simplifying Foster Care, Empowering Families** beneath the wordmark
- a five-part circular symbol above the wordmark
- the symbol uses the current brand palette with rounded, human-looking shapes

## Variants observed

### Wordmark variant

A wordmark-only version was provided showing:
- black background
- **FosterHub** without the circular icon
- the **F** highlighted in blue
- the **H** highlighted in green
- the remaining letters in dark gray

This version feels cleaner and more app-like, and may work well for headers, splash screens, or compact brand placements.

### Monochrome dark wordmark

A very subtle dark-gray-on-black wordmark version was also provided:
- no icon
- no tagline
- low-contrast monochrome treatment

This version feels sleek and understated, but likely works best as a presentation mockup or premium visual treatment rather than the primary production mark, because readability is weaker at smaller sizes.

### FH monogram

A compact monogram version was provided showing:
- black background
- blue **F**
- green **H**
- no full wordmark
- no tagline

This feels like the strongest candidate for an app icon, favicon, profile image, or compact social/avatar use.

### Full logo with people-circle symbol

Two additional variants were provided featuring a multi-person circular symbol above the wordmark:
- black background
- symbol made of five abstract people/forms in teal, blue, pink, orange, and green
- white/light wordmark: **FosterHub**
- one version with symbol + wordmark
- one version with symbol only

This is the strongest brand system so far because it gives FosterHub both:
- a memorable standalone icon
- a readable full lockup for website/header use

The circular arrangement communicates community, care, connection, and support very naturally — a good semantic fit for the FosterHub name.

## Visual impression

- modern
- soft and approachable
- community oriented
- feels like connected people forming a hub
